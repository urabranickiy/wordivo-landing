The science

Built on 140 years
of memory research

Spaced repetition isn't a gimmick. It's the most studied learning technique in cognitive psychology — and Wordivo applies it automatically to every word your students learn.

From Ebbinghaus to your students

In 1885, Hermann Ebbinghaus discovered the forgetting curve — we lose around 70% of new information within 24 hours. The solution? Review at increasing intervals, just before you'd forget.

Wordivo's 8-stage algorithm applies the same memory science used by flashcard apps trusted by millions of medical students and language learners worldwide.
